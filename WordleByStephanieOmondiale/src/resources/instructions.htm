How To Play Wordle

You have six tries to guess the five-letter secret word. The secret word may contain duplicate letters, like "hello".

After each guess, the color of the letter tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the actual secret word.

You can use the keyboard display to type your word or you can use the actual keyboard. After a successful guess or six unsuccessful guesses, you'll see a Statistics dialog. Pressing the Enter key will allow you to guess the next word. Pressing the Esc key will close the Statistics dialog and the Wordle game.

Examples

A gray tile indicates a letter that is not in the secret word.

None of the letters are in the secret word in any spot.

A yellow tile indicates a letter that is in the secret word, but in the incorrect position.

The L and E are in the secret word but not in the correct position.

A green tile indicates a letter that is in the secret word and is in the correct position.

The P is in the correct position and the E is in the secrent word but not in the correct position.

Good luck!
